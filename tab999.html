How To Use

Click on the smiley to copy BBCode to clipboard then go to forum's editor and Paste code there. Note: your forum should support BBCode.

About

This is a collection of old smiles mostly used in persian forums.

Special thanks to PersianTools members for providing and collecting smiles. Some of smiles collected from AsefSoft QuickSmiles, Worldofugly.de, BlackMice and other free smiles and forum websites.

This collection is for free use but if you have any complaints, please contact us here: https://github.com/retrosmiles/retrosmiles/issues/
